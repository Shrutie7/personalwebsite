Design and analysis of V4 solenoid engine

Based on Induction principle, which will be an alternate option for electric motor operated engine in near future, due to its high sustainability against fluctuating load as compared to motor driven electric engine. The model of V4 solenoid engine has been designed using Unigraphics NX & Creo and has been assembled.Analysis is being carried out on parts of V4 solenoid engine including spool, solenoid and connecting rod on Ansys. Based on different Ansys modules, and other softwares namely FEMM, OriginPro, design of the engine is optimized such that the cost of manufacturing is found to be Rs. 747.8439 obtained by comparing the model of V4 solenoid engine with traditional motors.
